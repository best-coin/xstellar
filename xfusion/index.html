How It Works

Getting started with XFusion and Stellar blockchain is simple and straightforward.

1

Create a Wallet

Download and set up a Stellar wallet like StellarTerm or connect your Ledger hardware wallet for maximum security.

2

Fund Your Account

Deposit lumens (XLM) or other supported assets to your wallet from exchanges or other Stellar addresses.

Start trading, creating, or managing assets with our intuitive interface and powerful tools.
